Who we are

EXELON specializes in switchgear manufacturing and panel building.


EXELON also provides custom metal fabrication services, including engineering and product development capabilities.

We have specialized in manufacturing custom and standard IT racks.

Today, the company is a go -to solutions partner for customers.

We have also sister companies that compliments with our products: Metallfit, Inc. (metal manufacturing company) and Datashield OEM Solutions (data center solutions).

Our Values

WHO WE ARE: We provide services to the Power Generation and Distribution, Telecommunication, Industrial and Commercial industries. We understand that the need for a safe and reliable power supply solutions are paramount. So we emphasize providing exceptional quality and uncompromised safety at a competitive price point. Finally, we pride ourselves on excellent customer orientation, so your required specifications, budgets and project deadlines are our main concern.


MISSION: We are committed to constant improvement in every aspect of our business price and quality for our clients, innovation to support ever-changing industry needs, and employee satisfaction to provide opportunity for personal and professional growth.


VISION: To be a go-to Power Supply Solutions partner in the Philippines.
